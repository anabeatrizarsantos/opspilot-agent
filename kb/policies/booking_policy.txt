Booking Policy:

Appointments must be scheduled in advance.

Cancellation Policy:

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the appointment.

Late cancellations may result in a cancellation fee.

Late Arrival:

Clients arriving more than 15 minutes late may need to reschedule.

No-Show Policy:

Failure to attend an appointment without notice may result in restricted future bookings.

For bridal and large services, a deposit may be required.
